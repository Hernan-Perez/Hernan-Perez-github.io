Forklift Simulator

Project developed by Delta3, customizing different versions for multiple companies by adapting forklift exercises and meticulously constructing virtual replicas of their warehouses.
Working closely with a 3D artist, my role primarily involved scene construction and exercise development, tailored to meet each client's unique needs and specifications.
The outcome is a comprehensive training platform designed not only to enhance operator proficiency and safety standards but also to serve evaluation purposes across diverse industrial environments.

The project was made in Unity and tested with HTC Vive Pro 2.
