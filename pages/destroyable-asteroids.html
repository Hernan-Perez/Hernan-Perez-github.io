Destroyable Worlds

VR Game made with Unity where the player has to defend a broken spaceship from incoming asteroids.
The player assumes the role of an alien positioned atop the spaceship, equipped with the ability to utilize four distinct skills to either break or divert asteroids. Over time, asteroids progressively increase in velocity, presenting an escalating challenge. Additionally, intermittent appearance of floating boxes in space provides an opportunity to repair the ship upon their destruction.

The current demo build needs SteamVR to work.
